Welcome to Vardhaman College Of Engineering ACM Student Chapter VCE

We are a community of students who are passionate about computer science and technology, and we are committed to promoting and advancing the field through various activities and events.

  • Guest lectures and talks from industry experts and academics
  • Workshops and training sessions on cutting-edge technologies and tools
  • Hackathons, coding competitions, and other programming challenges
  • Networking events with fellow students, faculty members, and industry professionals
  • Opportunities to participate in research projects and collaborate on innovative ideas

In addition to these activities, we also provide resources and support for students who are interested in pursuing careers in computer science and related fields. Whether you are just starting out or are looking to take your skills to the next level, we are here to help you achieve your goals.
